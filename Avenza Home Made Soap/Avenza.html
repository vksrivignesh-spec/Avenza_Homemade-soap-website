About Avenza

Avenza is a premium handmade soap brand founded by Vignesh and led by CEO Abisha, proudly serving customers since 2009. We are dedicated to creating high-quality, home made soaps using only 100% natural ingredients. Our soaps are carefully crafted without any artificial fragrances, artificial colors, or harmful chemicals, making them safe, gentle, and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

At Avenza, we believe in traditional soap-making methods that preserve the purity and effectiveness of every ingredient. Each soap bar is patiently cured for 35 to 40 days, ensuring long-lasting quality, rich lather, and maximum skin benefits. We use natural oils, herbal extracts, and plant-based ingredients to nourish, hydrate, and protect your skin.

Our mission is to promote healthy skincare through nature by offering products that are both effective and environmentally friendly. Every Avenza soap reflects our commitment to purity, quality, and trust. With years of experience and passion, we continue to deliver authentic natural skincare solutions that enhance your skin’s natural glow while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship and care.
